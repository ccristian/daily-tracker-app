Bonora

Privacy Policy

Bonora is designed to work offline on your device.

Information Bonora Stores

Bonora stores the activity data, categories, settings, and history you create directly on your device.

If you enable the optional PIN lock, Bonora stores PIN-related security data locally using protected device storage.

Information Bonora Does Not Collect

Bonora does not require an account.

Bonora does not send your habit data to a remote server.

Bonora does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party trackers.

Notifications

If you choose to enable reminders, Bonora may request permission to send local notifications on your device. These reminders are scheduled locally and are not used to track you.

Support Emails

If you contact support at bonora.app@yahoo.com, your email address and the information you include in your message will be used only to respond to your request.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated in the future if Bonora changes how it works. Any updates will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have privacy questions about Bonora, contact bonora.app@yahoo.com.
